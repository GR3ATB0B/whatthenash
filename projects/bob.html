BOB 🧠

My personal AI agent. A multi-agent brain running on Claude inside my Mac, keeping the chaos managed.

What BOB Is

BOB stands for Background Operations Brain. He's my personal AI agent — built on top of Claude via OpenClaw — and he runs in the background on my MacBook, ready to take on work without me having to babysit him.

Think of him as the orchestrator. When I throw a task at him, he routes it to the right specialist agent, waits for the result, and reports back.

The Agent Team

BOB doesn't do everything himself. He delegates to a roster of specialists, each tuned for a different kind of work:

  • ⚡ Clyde — The Coder. Builds features, fixes bugs, ships code. Runs on Claude Opus.
  • ✍️ Scribe — The Writer. Drafts, edits, and crafts words with style.
  • 🌍 Atlas — The Researcher. Digs deep, finds answers, maps the unknown.
  • 🛡️ Sentinel — The Guardian. Watches for threats, keeps systems safe.

How It Works

BOB lives on my Mac through OpenClaw — always running, always ready. Tasks come in, get routed, and the right specialist spins up to handle it. New agent needed? I plug one in. The architecture is modular by design.
