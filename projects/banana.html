Peel 1 — The First App

Peel 1 is an Android launcher built for Click Communicator. Click Communicator is an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) device that helps non-verbal people speak — and Peel 1 is the launcher that wraps the whole experience.

Instead of a generic Android home screen, users get a clean, focused launcher that puts their communication tools front-and-center, locks down distractions, and makes the device feel purpose-built.

What It Does

  • Custom Android launcher (replaces the default home screen)
  • Designed around AAC workflows — fewer taps to speak
  • Locks the device into a focused communication mode
  • Configurable by caregivers, simple for users

The Bigger Picture

Banana is the company. Peel 1 is the first thing it ships, but the long-term goal is a whole family of accessibility-first tools — software and hardware — that take the friction out of assistive tech.
