Hobby

Weightlifting 🏋️

Started in football, now it's a lifestyle.

About

Weightlifting started for me in football — power cleans, squats, deadlifts, and bench press were the core of our training. But even after football, I kept going. It's become a daily part of my routine.

The big four lifts are still my foundation: power clean, squat, deadlift, and bench press. I track everything with the Sporter app. There's something about moving heavy weight that clears the mind.

The Numbers

Football maxes (all in pounds):

  • 🏋️ Squat: 475–490 lbs
  • 💪 Bench: ~260 lbs
  • ⚡ Power Clean: 235 lbs

The Health Journey

Recently I've been on a bigger health journey beyond just lifting — zone 2 cardio, a whole foods diet (if it has a mother, I can eat it), and overall just taking better care of myself. It's not just about the numbers anymore.
