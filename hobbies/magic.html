The Cousin Tradition

Magic is a family tradition for me. My cousins and I learn tricks independently, then teach each other and present them whenever we get together. It's become this awesome tradition — everyone shows up with something new.

I use a normal deck of cards — no gimmicks, no special props. Just sleight of hand and misdirection.
