Hobby

Photography 📷

A mix of film and digital — capturing the world one frame at a time.

My Setup

I shoot on a Nikon D7000 DSLR — a hand-me-down kit from my mom that I've grown to love. I also shoot film with a vintage rangefinder camera and a SMC Pentax-A 50mm f/1.7 lens. I shoot mostly Kodak film, though I also use Fujifilm 200.

There's something about film that digital just can't replicate — the grain, the colors, the intentionality of having limited shots.

Gear

  • Nikon D7000 — Digital DSLR (mom's hand-me-down)
  • Vintage Rangefinder — Film camera, silver/chrome body
  • SMC Pentax-A 50mm f/1.7 — Manual focus prime lens
  • Kodak film — 35mm color film (primary stock)
  • Fujifilm 200 — 35mm color film (occasional)
